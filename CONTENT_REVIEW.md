# Content Review Notes

The source A1 and A2 files were Polish-oriented lesson data. The production lesson data has been converted to Central Catalan with English learner support.

## Adaptation Approach

- Replaced the `pl` field with `ca`.
- Preserved English meanings and translations where appropriate.
- Rewrote notes to describe Catalan usage, politeness, gender/article clues, currency, and context.
- Replaced Poland-specific references with Catalonia/Spain-relevant wording.
- Preserved the original A1/A2 topic and card counts.

## Cultural Changes

- Currency changed from zloty/grosz to euro/cèntim.
- Civic/admin vocabulary changed from PESEL-style references to NIE, DNI, appointments, documents, and local office language useful in Catalonia/Spain.
- Transport examples were generalized around metro, bus, tram, station, and city-centre wording.
- Food examples were adapted to Catalan items such as canelons and botifarra amb mongetes where the original content was specifically Polish.
- Names and streets were changed to Catalan/Barcelona-style examples.

## Native-Speaker Review Recommended

- Generated Catalan examples should receive a final native-speaker pass for idiom, register, and weak-pronoun naturalness.
- Administrative and health cards should be reviewed for phrasing that is useful but not overly specific legally or medically.
- Audio pronunciation depends on installed system voices and should be checked on target iPhone and Android devices.
